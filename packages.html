Overview

Three clear construction standards.

01

Basic Package

Rs. 2500 / Sq.ft

A practical package for essential construction needs with reliable structural quality.

  • 2D floor plan and 3D elevation design
  • Maha cement, DSRM steel and M20 RCC
  • Isolated footing up to 5 feet depth
  • Standard vitrified and antiskid tile allowances
  • Asian Paints base finish ranges

Essential Package

Rs. 2700 / Sq.ft

A balanced package with better structural details, improved materials and enhanced finish options.

  • Structural design included
  • Dalmia cement, Aishwaryam steel and M25 RCC
  • Dr.Fixit Bitufix foundation waterproofing
  • Improved tile, kitchen and bathroom allowances
  • Digital lock, Legrand switches and CCTV wiring
Enquire Essential

03

Luxury Package

Rs. 3000 / Sq.ft

A premium package with detailed drawings, upgraded fittings, higher finish values and luxury specifications.

  • Full drawing set including electrical, plumbing and carpentry
  • UltraTech cement, JSW steel and M25 RCC
  • 11 feet FFL to FFL ceiling height
  • Balharshah teak and luxury joinery values
  • EV, inverter, solar, CCTV and premium plumbing provisions
